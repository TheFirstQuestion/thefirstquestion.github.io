You tell him that the tomb is buried deep underground, impossible to find.

The Horus nods. Darkness fills the shaft only a few feet below the surface. He cannot see the stately chair and bed his father, Snefru, made for his mother, nor can he catch a glimpse of the white sarcophagus. But he knew they were there. It never entered his head that they might not be.

That night, you collapse onto you bed in relief. You did it! You actually did it! You managed to survive the day, and fix everything just in the nick of time. Again.
