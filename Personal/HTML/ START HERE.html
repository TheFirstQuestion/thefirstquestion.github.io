The year is 2563 BCE.

The great Horus is Khufu, and he has ruled the land for 60 years. You are his grand vizier, at his side for all of them.

You're distantly related to the royal house, but that's not how you've held the highest appointed position in the land for so long. Your job is to keep the country running smoothly, and to take care of the Horus's every need.

You're a shrewd, capable man, with many years of experience in your position. You've saved the day in the nick of time frequently over the years.

Are you ready to do it again? Yes!
