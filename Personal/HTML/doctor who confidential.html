Doctor Who Confidential

Background

Doctor Who Confidential was the companion behind-the-scenes show to the new series of Doctor Who. After each Doctor Who episode premiered on BBC One, its accompanying Confidential episode aired on BBC Three.

Confidential was cancelled at the end of Series Six in 2011 due to budget reasons. For Series Eight, the BBC began a new internet-based series called Doctor Who Extra. This is similar, though shorter.

Each Confidential episode was approximately 45 minutes, although episodes were cut down for release on the DVD series box sets. These "Confidential Cut Downs" were usually about 10-15 minutes long. Doctor Who Extra generally lasts 10 minutes.
