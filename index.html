The home of projects, tutorials, and other helpful things

Welcome to my website! Click on a link to be taken to the appropriate tool or project.

Civil War Map

An interactive map of major Civil War battles. Click on a box to zoom in on that area; hover over a dot to see information and a summary of a battle.

Finals Grade Calculator

Enter your grade in a class to find out how well you need to do on the final to get the grade you want.

Latin Verb Game

This game randomly conjugates a Latin verb. Your job is to figure out the tense, person, number, and voice.

To Do List

This tool allows you to create a custom, rearrangeable to do list. Note: Your list will disappear if you close the window.

Doctor Who Episode List

This is a sortable, jumpable list of every episode of Doctor Who and its spin-offs, including minisodes. Coming soon: links to watch each episode!
